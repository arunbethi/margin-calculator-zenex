Core Formulas
How every value is calculated
MSP Fee $
MSP Fee $ = Bill Rate × (MSP% ÷ 100)
Dollar amount deducted from bill rate and paid to the MSP vendor for managing the staffing contract.
Loading Cost $
Loading Cost $ = Bill Rate × (Loading% ÷ 100)
Covers employer burden: payroll taxes (FICA, FUTA, SUTA), workers comp, general liability, benefits, and overhead costs.
Gross Spread
Spread $ = Bill Rate − Pay Rate
Raw difference before fees. This is the maximum possible margin before deducting MSP fee and loading cost.
Net Margin $ ← Key Figure
Net Margin = Bill Rate − Pay Rate − MSP Fee − Loading Cost
The actual dollar profit per hour after all deductions. This is Zenex's per-hour revenue on the placement.
Margin %
Margin % = (Net Margin ÷ Bill Rate) × 100
Net margin as a percentage of the bill rate. Used to measure the health and efficiency of a placement.

Field Definitions
What each input means
Bill Rate
The hourly rate charged to the client/facility. This is the top-line revenue figure. All percentages (MSP, Loading) are calculated off this number.
Pay Rate
The hourly rate paid to the healthcare professional. Includes base taxable hourly wage only — does not include stipends or allowances.
MSP Fee %
Managed Service Provider fee. A % of bill rate paid to the MSP platform (AYA, SHC, AWS, etc.) for managing the contract. Reduces net margin directly.
Loading Cost %
Employer burden and overhead. Covers FICA (7.65%), FUTA/SUTA, workers comp, general liability insurance, benefits admin, and company overhead. Default: 15%.
Gross Spread
Bill Rate minus Pay Rate. Auto-calculated. Shows maximum available margin before fees are applied. Read-only field.

Travel Nurse — Weekly Calculations
Allowances and gross pay breakdown
Taxable Weekly Pay
Taxable = (Reg Hours × Pay Rate) + (OT Hours × Pay Rate × OT Multiplier)
Standard hours are paid at base pay rate. Overtime hours are multiplied by the OT rate (default 1.5x). This amount is subject to federal and state income tax.
Non-Taxable Weekly
Non-Taxable = Lodging + Meals (weekly)
Tax-free stipends paid to cover assignment-related expenses. Must not exceed IRS/GSA per diem limits for the assignment location.
Total Weekly Gross
Total Gross = Taxable Weekly + Non-Taxable Weekly
The full weekly gross the candidate receives, combining both taxable wages and non-taxable allowances.
Loading + Meals Weekly
Load + Meals = Loading Cost $ + Meals Weekly
Combined weekly employer cost of burden (loading) and meal stipend. Useful for internal cost analysis per placement week.

Tips & Best Practices
Getting the most from this calculator
🎯
Target 10%+ margin on all placements. Below 10% leaves very little buffer for overtime, pay corrections, or client billing disputes.
📈
Use the MSP Comparison Table to quickly see how margin changes across all vendor options. Useful when negotiating bill rates with facilities.
⚠️
High MSP fee vendors (AWS 6%, Favorite 7%) require a higher bill rate to maintain healthy margin. Always verify bill rate before submitting to these MSPs.
🏥
For travel nurses, always check GSA per diem rates for the assignment city before setting lodging and meal stipends to stay IRS-compliant.
🔢
Loading cost of 15% is Zenex's standard. Adjust upward (16–18%) for assignments with higher workers comp risk classifications (ICU, ER, OR).
💰
Net Margin below $5.00/hr is flagged red as a hard warning — at this level, any payroll correction or compliance cost can push the placement into a loss.
